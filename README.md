# Performance Shake

## What You Need
- High-speed blender (Vitamix 5200 or equivalent with 64oz container)
- Kitchen scale (grams)
- 14 titanium containers (7 large for produce, 7 small for powder)
- Large thermos or drinking container (2L+)
- Small glass for broccoli sprout shot

---

## Weekly Prep (30 min, once a week)

### Beets (side item — eat separately)
Peel, cube into bite-size pieces. Cook using one of:
- **Air fryer (best):** 375F for 15-20 min, toss halfway. Caramelized edges, quick.
- **Microwave (fastest):** 4-5 min with a splash of water, covered.
- **Oven (bulk):** Wrap whole beets in foil, 400F for 60 min, then peel and cube.

Store cooked cubes in fridge for the week. Portion 100g per day.

### Watermelon
Cube whole melon into ~1" pieces. Spread on parchment-lined sheet pan, freeze 2-3 hours, bag into 220g portions. Lasts 5+ weeks in freezer.

### Avocados
Cube 5-6 ripe avocados, spread on sheet, freeze, bag into 120g portions.

### Frozen Produce — make 7 containers
Into each large titanium container, measure:
- 220g berry medley
- 220g watermelon (from frozen prep)
- 120g avocado (from frozen prep)
- 60g frozen kale (strip stems — use leafy parts only)
- 50g frozen spinach

Freeze all 7 containers.

**Stored separately** (not in frozen container):
- 50g macadamia halves — room temp or fridge
- 1 medjool date — room temp, pitted

### Powder — make 7 containers
Into each small titanium container, measure:
- 105g egg white protein powder
- 30g whey protein isolate
- 50g cacao powder (full-fat, non-defatted)
- 25g red lentil flour
- 20g sunflower lecithin
- 15g collagen peptides
- 10g green banana flour
- 10g creatine
- 5g acacia fiber
- 3g chicory root inulin
- 2g ceylon cinnamon
- 1.5g taurine
- 1g salt
- 1g instant coffee *(optional — chocolate flavor booster)*

Seal and store at room temp.

### Keep these 2 separate (add fresh each morning)
These oxidize or clump if pre-mixed:
- **5g matcha** — EGCG degrades with air exposure
- **7g psyllium husk** — absorbs moisture and clumps everything

### Side items — keep separate
These are removed from the shake for taste/texture but still eaten daily. Skipping them has real downsides:

- **5g broccoli sprout powder** — take as a water shot
  *If skipped: lose sulforaphane (Nrf2 activator, strongest anticancer compound in the shake)*
- **0.5g mustard seed powder** — add to broccoli sprout shot
  *If skipped: lose myrosinase enzyme that activates the sulforaphane above — without it, broccoli sprout powder is ~80% less effective*
- **15g goji berries** — eat dry as a snack
  *If skipped: lose zeaxanthin (eye/retina protection — critical with no sunlight exposure), unique polysaccharides, and beta-carotene*
- **100g cooked beet** — eat as a side snack with a pinch of salt
  *If skipped: lose dietary nitrate (~280mg, boosts blood flow + exercise performance), betalains (anti-inflammatory), and folate*
- **1 astaxanthin softgel** — swallow with water (do NOT blend — sharp sour taste)
  *If skipped: lose astaxanthin (strongest carotenoid antioxidant, 6000x stronger than vitamin C, eye/skin/joint protection)*

## Every Morning (~5 minutes)

Put blender on scale. Add everything in this order (tare between items or track running total):

### 1. Liquids (bottom)
- 480ml milk
- 45ml EVOO
- 5ml vanilla extract

### 2. Soft/hard items
- 50g macadamia halves
- 1 medjool date (pitted)
- 20g allulose
- 2.5g monk fruit liquid

### 3. Frozen produce
- Dump frozen produce container (berries, watermelon, avocado, kale, spinach)

### 4. Powders (top)
- Dump powder container (cacao, proteins, creatine, lentil flour, etc.)
- Add fresh: 5g matcha + 7g psyllium **last**

**Blend high speed 90-120 seconds until fully smooth.**

### Check consistency
- Too thick → add water (50-100ml)
- Too thin → wait 2 min (psyllium gels)

Pour into thermos or drink straight from blender container.

### 5. Side items
- **Broccoli sprout shot:** 5g broccoli sprout powder + 0.5g mustard seed powder in a small glass of water. Stir, shoot it.
- **Goji berries:** eat 15g dry as a snack.
- **Beet:** eat 100g cooked beet cubes with a pinch of salt.
- **Astaxanthin:** swallow 1 softgel with water.

---

## Night-Before Option
Make the full shake at night, but save these 2 for morning:
- 5g matcha
- 7g psyllium husk

Store the base in the fridge. In the morning, add the 2 fresh powders and re-blend 30 seconds. Good for up to 24 hours refrigerated.

Side items (broccoli sprout shot, goji, beet) always fresh in the morning.

---

## How to Drink
- Wait 20-30 min after waking, drink a glass of water first
- Sip over 1-3 hours — do not chug
- Drink water alongside the shake — minimum 2L water beyond the shake daily
- Stir occasionally — psyllium thickens over time
- This is a meal (~2,900 cal, ~130g protein)

---

## First 4 Weeks — Fiber Ramp-Up

Your gut needs time to handle ~50g fiber. Adjust the POWDER container during ramp weeks:

### Week 1 — reduce these in powder container:
- Psyllium: 7g → 3g
- Acacia: 5g → 2g
- Green banana flour: skip (0g)
- Everything else: full dose

Expect: bloating, gas, loose stools days 1-3. Worst week.
If problems: drop inulin to 1.5g, reduce kale to 40g. Ginger tea helps.

### Week 2 — increase:
- Psyllium: 3g → 5g
- Acacia: 2g → 3g
- Green banana flour: 0g → 5g

Expect: 50% less bloating. Stools firming.

### Week 3 — almost there:
- Psyllium: 5g → 7g (full)
- Acacia: 3g → 5g (full)
- Green banana flour: 5g → 7g

Expect: most symptoms resolved.

### Week 4+ — full dose
Everything at full amounts. Fully adapted.

If you miss multiple days: re-ramp at week 2 doses for 3 days.
After antibiotics: re-ramp from week 1.

## Rules
- Drink 2L+ water beyond the shake daily
- Take probiotic (Seed DS-01) daily, especially weeks 1-2
- If any week is rough, hold that dose an extra week — no rush
- Do not heat above 70C (degrades matcha + broccoli sprout compounds)
- Sip slowly — chugging high-fiber shake causes GI distress
- Monitor heart rate if combining high cacao doses with stimulant medication + matcha
